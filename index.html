History of India

Ancient India

India has a rich and ancient history that dates back over 5,000 years. The Indus Valley Civilization was one of the world's earliest urban civilizations.

Medieval India

During the medieval period, powerful kingdoms and empires like the Maurya, Gupta, Delhi Sultanate, and Mughals ruled the land, bringing advancements in art, architecture, and culture.

Modern India

In the 18th century, India came under British rule. After years of struggle and the leadership of Mahatma Gandhi and others, India gained independence on August 15, 1947.
